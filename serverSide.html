About Server Side

Up to this point you have developed static content and focused on the client side of web development. This course we will expand on that knowledge and focus on creating dynamic web sites and application with the introduction of Server-Side Web Development. Throughout the course you will learn the PHP Scripting Language, the most widely used server-side development language, and MySQL, one of the most widely used, open source relational database management systems. Together you will be able to build dynamic web sites, securely track users interactions, and even create your own content management system.

Throughout the course, we will explore the benefits and capabilities of PHP and MySQL through a series of projects ranging from PHP Templates and Image Galleries to Appointment Reservation Systems. Projects will be developed step by step, in class, and new concept and techniques will be demonstrated.

Prerequisites: MTM6230
