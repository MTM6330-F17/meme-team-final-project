About Hussein Tayrani

Hussein is currently a professor at Algonquin College in the School of Media and Design. He teaches Design, Video and Motion Graphics courses for IMD. Helping his students to thrive and become professionals at work are among his main goals.

Hussein's drive is powered by a special fuel he identifies as his 'Coffeetivity', the combination of coffee with creativity. When his colleagues see him tackling a project with a cup of coffee in his hand, there is no doubt he is up to something genius. On a regular day, if not teaching or working on Motion Graphics projects, Hussein is watching, playing or reading news about soccer.
